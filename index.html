INSTRUCTIONS

Goal

The object of the game is to guess the Unknown Character, who was randomly chosen from the panel of ten characters.

In order to make your best guess, type questions about the Unknown Character's traits into the input and submit to reveal a clue.

How to Ask Questions

The format of your questions is important! You must be sure to include a valid trait, as well as a description of that trait.

And of course, always end your questions with a question mark!

Key

Valid Traits:
| Gender | Hair | Eyes | Glasses | Facial Hair | Bald | Jewelry |

Example Questions:
"Is their gender girl?"
"Do they have blonde hair?"
"Do they have green eyes?"
"Do they wear glasses?"
"Do they have facial hair?"
"Are they bald?"
"Are they wearing jewelry?"
